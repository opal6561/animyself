Code of Conduct

AniMyself's online community is designed for all ages. Therefore, all videos must abide with the rules posted below. Emphasis will be marked in italics.

NOTE: The Code of Conduct is a modification of, and in addition to, the Terms of Use and Privacy Policy, and should be understandable by all ages.

The Code of Conduct was last modified: 24 Jan 2021.


What's Not Allowed

Violent and Graphic Content

Some videos on AniMyself may contain fights, attacks, and/or deaths. While these types of videos are okay on AniMyself, videos that depict the following are not allowed:

  • Mass blood loss or mutilation;
  • Animal attacks;
  • Injury or killing by vehicles;
  • Amputations or beheadings;
  • The Attack on Pearl Harbor, The September 11 Attacks, bombings, or school shootings, except for educational use; or
  • First-person stories of committing crimes.

Sexual Content

Suggestive dialogue, kissing, and love scenes are permitted on AniMyself. Videos that depict the following are not allowed:

  • Sexual slang;
  • Masturbation or fondling;
  • Depiction of breasts, genitalia, and buttocks;
  • Usage of "sex toys"; and
  • Sexual abuse.

Harassment and Cyberbullying

Harassing, cyberbullying, threatening, and defamation are never tolerated on AniMyself in any way, shape, or form. Saying, "I was just joking," is not an acceptable excuse for such behavior.

Hate Speech

Hate speech is never tolerated on AniMyself. The definition of "hate speech" includes videos that promote violence or hatred against persons based on:

  • Age;
  • Caste;
  • Disability;
  • Ethnicity;
  • Gender;
  • Nationality;
  • Race;
  • Immigration Status;
  • Religion; or
  • Sexual Orientation.

NOTE: This list is not exhaustive.

In addition, videos that depict Confederate flags, swastikas, Ku Klux Klan symbols, Nazi Germany concentration camps, burning of crosses, or police violence against minorities are strictly prohibited on AniMyself, and will immediately result in an account suspension (see below), unless for educational purposes.

Spam, Announcements, and Advertising

AniMyself is not a chatroom! Therefore, there are various types of videos that are prohibited on AniMyself:

  • Blank videos (cannot be shared);
  • Scoreboards and results;
  • Announcement videos;
  • Signup videos; and
  • Videos intended to manipulate the community statistics (like boosting a user's video count or getting on the community leaderboards).

In order to prevent new users from flooding the community home page, they have to wait 30 minutes between sharing videos, or otherwise it will be flooded with a single user's videos.

Grounded Videos

Videos associated with the "X Gets Grounded" community, such as grounded videos, punishment days, behavior card days, and Barney errors are strictly forbidden on AniMyself. This website is not GoAnimate!


Consequences of Inappropriate Behavior

Warnings and Suspensions

In the event a moderator finds that a video has violated the Code of Conduct or Terms of Use, they will remove the video immediately. Depending on the number of prior offenses and the severity of the offense, the account that created it will either receive a warning or be temporarily suspended:

  1. Warning
  2. 3-day suspension
  3. 7-day suspension
  4. 10-day suspension
  5. 14-day suspension

NOTE: In the event of more serious offenses, an account may be suspended for a longer period of time.

Account Terminations

AniMyself has the right to terminate your account at any time and for any reason, even without prior warning. Generally, an account can be terminated for:

  • Repeated violations of the Code of Conduct or Terms of Use;
  • A single serious offense (such as predatory behavior, spam, or phishing); and
  • Being dedicated to a policy violation (like hate speech, impersonation, and cyberbullying).

NOTE: In some cases, terminated users will also be prevented from creating new accounts via their IP address and/or email address.

Moderator Policies

All moderators strive to be fair and unbiased towards all users, even though it may not seem like that to user who are being warned or suspended. All decisions regarding Code of Conduct violations are made by the moderators.


Appealing

Procedure

If you believe your suspension or termination is unfair or biased, you may submit up to three appeals by contacting appeals@animyself.com. Your appeal must include:

  • Your username;
  • Your email address;
  • The moderator that suspended your account; and
  • The reason why you believe your suspension was unfair or biased in as much detail as possible.

Upon receiving an account suspension appeal, the person you appeal to will respond to your appeal within twenty-four (24) hours. If you are not satisfied with the decision, you may appeal to higher authority (see below).

Multiple Appeals

The appealing order goes as follows:

  1. Other moderators,
  2. AniMyself employees, and
  3. The CEO of AniMyself.

For your final appeal to the CEO of AniMyself, you have more rights than other appeals, including the right to counsel and to call on witnesses.


Remember…

AniMyself is a place to animate and have fun, and that can only happen when all users follow the Code of Conduct and other policies.
