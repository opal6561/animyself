Video Ratings

Although AniMyself is designed for use by all ages, there are some videos that are aimed at a specific age range or unsuitable for younger audiences. Therefore, you must tell us which groups your videos are suitable for. While we cannot provide legal advice, the details below will help you decide what rating your videos should get.

Failure to give your videos an appropriate rating may cause consequences on AniMyself as well as legal consequences.


Ratings

G – General Audience

These videos contain little to no material that parents may find inappropriate for their children.


10+ – Suitable for Persons 10 and Over

These videos contain material that parents may find inappropriate for children under ten. Videos rated 10+ may contain:

  • Some fantasy or cartoon violence;
  • Crude humor;
  • Mild language;
  • Some suggestive scenes; and/or
  • Partial nudity.

13+ – Suitable for Persons 13 and Over

These videos contain material that parents may find inappropriate for children under thirteen. Videos rated 13+ may contain:

  • Strong violence;
  • Crude humor;
  • Some strong language;
  • Strong suggestive scenes; and/or
  • Partial nudity.

17+ – Suitable for Persons 17 and Over

These videos contain material that is explicit or graphic in nature, but do not violate the Code of Conduct or Terms of Use. You must be at least 17 years old to view these videos. These videos will also be hidden from users under 17 or using Safe Mode. Videos rated 17+ may contain:

  • Graphic violence that shows blood;
  • Dark humor;
  • Sexual activity;
  • Explicit language;
  • Substance abuse; and/or
  • Full nudity.
