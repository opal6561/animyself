Video Maker Features

Lip Sync

Making characters speak in AniMyself is easy. You can record audio from your microphone or create it with text-to-speech, then assign it to a character with the click of a button.

Unlimited Creation

Say goodbye to the limited, bland assets other video makers offer. AniMyself offers 50+ character designs, and X+ scenes, with various appearance for your videos' needs. Characters and scenes have full customization, and there is no limit to how many characters or scenes you can make.

Perspective

Tired of wasting time trying to layer and resize characters and props correctly? Those frustrations are gone, as AniMyself layers and resizes for you to give the appearance of depth.

Character Actions

You have full customization of characters' limbs, expression, and motion. They can hold props, move across the scene, and much more, in a smooth, professional manner.

Share and Export

Export videos directly to the Community, or to third-party websites for the world to see. You can also download your videos to your computer, and anyone can share or use your videos.

Video History

Did you make a mistake? Video History allows you to view each saved version of a video. This makes it easy to recover lost data or create a new video based on an old version.

Security and Privacy

AniMyself has high security standards and ensures a secure animation community worldwide. To protect users' data, we have comprehensive security processes and controls to comply with COPPA, GDPR, CCPA, and other certifications.
